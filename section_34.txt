ABANDONMENT. In the event that the Tenant(s) abandon(s) the Property the Landlord may declare the Lease terminated, recover possession of the Property, enter the premises, remove the Tenant’s belongings and lease the same to another without incurring any liability to the Tenant(s) for doing the same. In the event of the abandonment of the Property, the Landlord may recover from the Tenant(s) unpaid Rent until the Property is leased to another person or otherwise occupied by the Landlord or another under the Landlord’s right.
