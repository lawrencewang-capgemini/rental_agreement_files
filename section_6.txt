RENT C. Application of Payments. Whenever there are different sums owed by the Tenant(s) to the Landlord, any payment shall be applied first to those obligations other than Rent including but not limited to association/community dues, Late Fee, repairs chargeable to the Tenant(s), and other charges notwithstanding any notations or specifications made by the Tenant(s) on the application of any payment paid to the Landlord.
